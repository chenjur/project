Profile


Hi my name is Tenzin chenjur. I am a tibetan refugee and was born and raised in Bhutan. I live in a little industrial town called Bjemina which is around 25 km from the capital city Thimphu.




My qualification


I studied till the fourth grade in my home town(Bjemina community primary school). And then i got transferred to middle school(Khasadrapchu middle secondary school). I studied till the tenth grade there. Then i graduated and got into YHSS (Yangchenphug Higher Secondary School) the premier high school the country could offer. And now after two years my school days were finally over.

My interest


I love basketball. My favourite NBA team is Housten Rockets because my favourite basketball player is there. James Harden.
Other than basketball, i love coding. I love how beautiful things can be created just by typing some codes.
I love watching movies, animes and listening to musics.

Family History


Like i said i was born and raised in Bhutan. My mother is a Tibetan and my father a Bhutanese, a kheng to be exact. During the Chinese invasion in Tibet my grandparents fled to Bhutan. They stayed in Bumthang for a while where my mom was born. They soon later moved to Bjemina(our current residence). Thats where i was born.
